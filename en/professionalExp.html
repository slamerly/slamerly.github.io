🍷 Cellar Operator

Subject:

I worked as a Cellar Operator (Caviste) during the summer season at Domaine Royal de Jarras.
I was assigned to the transfer stage of the winemaking pipeline, right after the first fermentation.
My role was to transfer wine between tanks (from around 640 hL to tanks up to 5000 hL) and perform fining operations ("collage") according to production requirements.
Depending on pump configuration and batch volume, each transfer operation could last between 2 and 3 hours, requiring focus, patience, and consistency.
I also took samples for analysis by the cellar master and laboratory teams, ensuring clear handover and reliable process tracking.
Working in a 3-shift rotation (3x8), I developed strong teamwork, discipline, adaptability, and communication in a high-season production environment.

Worked on:

  • Tank-to-tank transfer operations
  • Fining operations (collage)
  • Monitoring of transfer cycles and flow control

  • Cross-shift communication and team coordination
  • Priority handling in high-season activity

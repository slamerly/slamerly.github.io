Masquerade

Subject:

Masquerade is my graduation project made in group.
It's a puzzle adventure game set in a deceptive theatre, in which you find yourself playing in a well-known play while trying to save your wife.
I had the privilege of being both, Producer and Lead Programmer.
I handled the migration of the project from GitHub to Perforce and integrated Wwise at the request of the Sound Designers.
This game has two specifics environement working differently, the theater's scene and the backstage. For the smooth running of the game, I needed to make specefic loading system for the two game zone and working well with progress sytem.

Worked on:

  • Project & Team Management
  • Product documentation
  • Sublevels loading systems
  • Progress system (save and checkpoints)
  • UI (Main menu, Debug menu and Pause menu)
  • Tools (Lights' Management)
  • WWISE integration

Details:

Release Date: May 2025
Language: English, French
Roles: Producer, Lead Programmer, Programmer
Production Time: 11 months
Engine: Unreal
Platform: PC

Squeaky Clean

Subject:

Squeaky Clean is a group project doned during the Global Game Jam 2025, themed "Bubbles".
The goal of the game is to satisfied the maximum of machines before the end of the timer.
Each machines has their own timer, you need to satisfied them by bring them the objects they want.
But becarefull, if you bring them the wrong object, they'll be unsitisfied and reduce the time to satisfied them.
Watch out about big plastic ducks, they are easy target you!

Worked on:

  • Interaction System
  • Game's Rules - Score & Timer
  • Machines' Spawner
  • Machines' Behavior
  • Machines' Timer
  • End Game - Scoreboard

Bas Haut Bab

Subject:

Bas Haut Bab is a group project was made at the reqest of Montpellier town hall, for the Coeur De Ville en Lumière Festival of 2023.
We had some specific constraints like the accessibility to a wide audience and be as fun to play as it was to watch.
We had to consider visual contraints, as the game was to be projected onto the façade of a historic building.
This is a coop game, whose the aim is to completed the more of sequences you can before the end of the timer.
You need to exchange you the gems to bring them to the right mask and game points.
At the end you have a Scoreboard to compare your performance with that of the other binomes.

Worked on:

  • Game and score systems
  • Creation and management of obstacles
  • Gems' spawner
  • Gems' physics
  • Tutorial

Details:

Release Date: November 2023
Language: French
Roles: Programmer
Production Time: 1 month
Engine: Unreal
Platform: PC

The Diig

Subject:

The DIIG is a group project which aims to make a remake of The Dig game of 1995.
